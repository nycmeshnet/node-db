Connecting Highs and Lows: Physical Infrastructure

Infrastructure is often far more ambitious than immediate realities. NYC Mesh, by nature of running on a volunteer-based labor structure, is hindered in its ability to connect all of its nodes in a timely fashion. This can lead to “phantom nodes” that have either been installed but not connected, requested but not installed, or otherwise poorly documented and barely in existence.
