The Height of the City: Physical Infrastructure

Because Mesh WiFi routers transmit connection through a direct line of sight, the growth of Mesh nodes are highly dependent on the building heights within the urban fabric. Tall buildings are especially necessary for hubs and supernodes that distribute internet to thousands of other nodes. NYC Mesh prioritizes tall buildings such as NYCHA buildings, skyscrapers, churches, schools, libraries, existing antenna masts, and tall apartment buildings.


Building Elevations

This visualization shows the sea-level elevation of each node along a temporal x-axis. Consistently throughout time, installations of NYC Mesh nodes, hubs, and supernodes have taken place on both low-elevation and high-elevation buildings.

The growth of NYC Mesh “embraces the fullness of hardware diversity and interconnectivity” to fill in the gaps present in incumbent ISP coverage (NYC Mesh, 2022).
